About Me

A Vision Rooted in Service and Science

I am a dedicated medical aspirant from the serene valleys of Khaplu, Baltistan — a region where the grandeur of nature meets the resilience of its people. My journey toward medicine is not merely a career choice, but a calling shaped by the healthcare challenges I witnessed in my community.

Having completed my foundational education at Public School and College Skardu, I have spent the past two years preparing intensively for medical entrance examinations in Bahawalpur. This period has strengthened my academic foundation and deepened my commitment to becoming a healthcare professional who can make a tangible difference.

"Medicine represents the convergence of scientific inquiry and compassionate service. My aspiration is to bridge the gap between advanced medical knowledge and the underserved communities of remote mountain regions, where geography should never determine the quality of healthcare one receives."
